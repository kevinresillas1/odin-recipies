Pizza Recipe


Description:


The combination of sauce, cheese, and any choice of toppings. When all combined, they make a delicious recipe famously known as Pizza! Originating from Central and Southern Italy. Pizza started to make it's appearance in Amercia in the 20th century.


Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Bread
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Add warm water, dry yeast, and olive oil into a bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center of the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Warp the dough in plastic and refridgerate, check to see if dough has risen.
  5. Once dough has risen, start to shape into big circle.
  6. Once shaped, add favorite toppings.
  7. Bake until golden brown.
  8. Take out of the oven, slice and enjoy!
