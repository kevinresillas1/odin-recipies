Steak Recipe:

Description:


A well cooked streak makes for a great meal at any time of the day! Choose the meat of your choice and apply your seasoning of choice. Cook the steak to your preferred wellness and you have yourself an easy meal!

  • Choice of meat
  • Seasoning of choice
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Side foods of choice

Steps:

  1. Heat your pan on high heat.
  2. Apply oil to the pan and evenly spread it.
  3. Once pan is hot, apply your meat and sear on each side for 1 minute.
  4. After searing for 1 minute, turn heat down to medium and cook 3 minutes on each side.
  5. After cooking each side for 3 minutes, turn off the flame and let steak soak for 1 minute.
  6. Once time has ended, you can take your steak out and serve on plate.
  7. Serve with your favorite sides of choice.
